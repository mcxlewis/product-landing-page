Established in Richmond, Virginia in 2019, Skull & Cross Stitch is an independently LGBTQ owned company, creating custom & commission based items as well as creating unique items for sale. Maddox Lewis, creator and founder, finds joy and comfort in creativity. They say,
"When my mind gets busy, my hands need to be busy as well. It only makes sense for something productive to come out of that. If I can create something from that compulsion, especially something that might have a function, isn't that great?"
