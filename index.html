thoughtfully made, handcrafted fiber arts from richmond, virginia.

crochet, knit, and embroidered crafts from our hands to yours.
established in richmond, virginia in 2019, skull & cross stitch is an independently lgbtq-owned company, constructing custom & commission based items as well as making unique pieces for sale. m. lewis, creator and founder, finds joy and comfort in creativity. lewis says,
"when my mind gets busy, my hands need to be busy as well. it only makes sense for something productive to come out of that. if I can create something from that feeling, especially something that might have a function, isn't that great?"
