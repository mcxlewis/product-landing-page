Handcrafted fiber arts from Richmond, Virginia.

Thoughtfully made, stitch by stitch.

Crochet, knit, and embroidered crafts from our hands to yours. Since 2019 and established in Richmond, Virginia, Skull & Cross Stitch has been an independently LGBTQ owned company, creating custom & commission based items as well as creating unique items for sale. Maddox Lewis, creator and founder, finds joy and comfort in creativity. They say,
When my mind gets busy, my hands need to be busy as well. It only makes sense for something productive to come out of that. If I can create something from that compulsion, especially something that might have a function, isn't that great?
